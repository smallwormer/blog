!

interface ens3

 ip ospf cost 100

 no link-detect

!

 no link-detect

!

interface lo

 no link-detect

!

router ospf

 ospf router-id 172.16.237.10

 log-adjacency-changes

 auto-cost reference-bandwidth 1000
